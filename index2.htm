Welcome to the 32cue Stepper/Sequencer Web Installer!

  1. Select the version of the hardware you have from the options below (probably HW Revision 3).
  2. Hit Install. A list with available COM ports will apear.
  3. TURN OFF YOUR Stepper/Sequencer (it must remain off for the duration of the update process)
  4. Plug in your device to a USB port on your computer.
    Connect to the port that appears in the list.
    If no new port appears, disconnect the device, hold down the knob on the stepper/sequencer, and reconnect.
  5. Press Install and wait for the installation to finish.
  6. DISCONNECT THE DEVICE FROM YOUR COMPUTER.
  7. Turn on your device and verify the verison that appears when the device boots matches the version you attempted to install.
